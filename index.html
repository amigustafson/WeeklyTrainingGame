What Day is it?

Monday

Squats! Go as deep as you can.

Tuesday

Tricep Dips. For added challenge, let one leg be up in the air.

Wednesday

Hip thrusts. The best way to get that peach. Try different variations of bridges, for range of motion do them in a reversed plank.

Thursday

Wide armed push-ups. Works both chest, shoulders and arms if done correctly!

Friday

Ab-tipsies. Keep you arms out to the sides (airplane arms) and tip slowly and with control, side to side. Breathe.
